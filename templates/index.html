Impact of Education Expenditures on Life Quality

The purpose of this project was to analyze how education investment impacts the quality of people's lives around the world. Our hypothesis is that the more a country invest in education, the higher the overall standard of living, and therefore a higher return on that investment. We expect to see four related attributes influenced by higher levels of investment.


We gathered data from the CIA World Factbook, using the last 13 years data from 250 countries.




Our site provides the source data and visualizations created as part of the analysis, as well as explantions and descriptions of trends and correlations we discovered.
